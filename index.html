About The Game

Bingo Betty throws you into the seedy underbelly of underground bingo tournaments where desperate seniors gamble their social security checks for a shot at glory. This isn't your grandmother's bingo night—it's a high-stakes roguelite where every draw could be your last.

Master the triple-match system where balls have three attributes: number, color, and symbol. Chain together devastating combos using "blotters"—special abilities that trigger cascading reactions across your card. Collect over 150 unique passive items, each with tag-based synergies that transform your playstyle.

Choose from six eccentric characters, each with different starting items, cards, and a distinct visual theme tied to their personality. Progress through escalating stages, face off against boss rounds, and unlock achievements as you climb the ranks of the most dangerous bingo circuit in town.

Key Features

High-Stakes Gambling

Risk it all in underground bingo tournaments. Manage your dwindling funds while chasing the big score.

Chain Reaction Combos

Trigger devastating chain reactions with blotters—special abilities that cascade across your card.

152 Passive Items

Discover a massive arsenal of items with tag-based synergies. Build broken combos and stack multipliers.

32 Bingo Cards

Unlock and collect different bingo card styles, each offering unique strategic opportunities.

Fever Mode

Ten levels of escalating difficulty with unique stacking modifiers. Beat the game to unlock the next fever level.

200 Achievements

Unlock achievements tied to progression, mastery, and discovery. Reveal new characters, items, and secrets.
